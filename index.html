From Danger

Restoring the Roots of the Land

At ALMASAR, we don't just clear mines; we reclaim the future. Our mission combines technical demining with agricultural rehabilitation.

By removing explosive remnants of war, we allow farmers to return to their fields. We plant olive trees—symbols of peace and resilience—where warning signs once stood. This creates a sustainable ecosystem that supports both nature and the local economy.
